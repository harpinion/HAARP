The weather data is measured locally at the same place as the HAARP radio waves.
This can be compared with HAARP activity.

Description of the columns:

"nk_wetter_ic";	Dataset-ID
"dt_timestamp";	Time of measurement
"ns_mbar";		air pressure
"ni_feucht_11_out";	air humidity (+50 cm)
"ns_temp_ds_erd";	temperature in the earth (-20 cm)
"ns_temp_ds_boden";	air temperature (+5 cm)
"ns_temp_ds_out";	air temperature (+50 cm)
"ns_cf_pm1_0";		air particle 0.3-1.0 µm concentration
"ns_cf_pm2_5";		air particle 1.0-2.5 µm concentration
"ns_cf_pm10";		air particle 2.5-10 µm concentration
"ni_cf_temp_11";	air temperature particle measurement
"ni_cf_feucht_11";	air humidity particle measurement
"ni_boden_feucht";	indicator of earth humidity
"ni_regen";		indicator for rain
"ns_regen";		rain measurement [mm]
"ns_wind_speed";	wind speed [m/s]

_______________________________________________________________________________

Diese Wetterdaten wurden am gleichen Platz lokal gemessen wo auch die HAARP Aktivität gemessen wird.
So können diese Wetterdaten mit der HAARP Aktivität verglichen werden.

Beschreibung der Datenbank-Felder:

"nk_wetter_ic";	Datensatz-ID
"dt_timestamp";	Zeitpunkt
"ns_mbar";		Luftdruck

"ns_temp_ds_out";	Allgemeine Termperatur (+50 cm)
